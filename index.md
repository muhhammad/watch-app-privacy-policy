# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 6 January 2026

## App Name
Watch Market Intelligence Tool

## Overview
Watch Market Intelligence Tool ("the App") is a data analytics application that uses official Meta APIs, including the Instagram Graph API and Facebook Pages API, to collect and analyze social media data for market intelligence purposes related to luxury watches.

The App does **not** use web scraping and does **not** access private user data.

---

## Information We Collect

The App may collect the following data **only via official Meta APIs**:

- Public Instagram post metadata (e.g., captions, timestamps)
- Engagement metrics (likes, comments count, reach, impressions)
- Aggregated insights from Instagram Business or Creator accounts
- Facebook Page metadata connected to Instagram Business accounts

The App **does not collect**:
- Private account data
- Personal messages (DMs)
- Login credentials
- Passwords or payment information
- Data from accounts not owned or authorized by the App user

---

## How We Use Information

Collected data is used exclusively to:
- Analyze market trends related to luxury watches
- Measure content performance and engagement patterns
- Generate aggregated analytics and reports
- Support research and business intelligence use cases

No automated decision-making or profiling of individuals is performed.
